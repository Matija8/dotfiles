You need to first setup ts imports, then workspaces.
1) TypeScript imports (tsconfig.json > references...)
2) Yarn/Npm Workspaces (package.json, node_modules...)
3) *Webpack (resolve > aliases)

TypeScript debugging commands:
tsc

Workspace debugging commands:
yarn workspaces list --json # ??
yarn workspaces info --json

https://classic.yarnpkg.com/en/docs/cli/workspaces

https://spin.atomicobject.com/2022/02/07/typescript-project-workspaces/

You need a tsconfig.json in your /shared workspace.
