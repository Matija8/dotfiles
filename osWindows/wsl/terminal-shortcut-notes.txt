Find the `wt.exe` app in:
    %LocalAppData%\Microsoft\WindowsApps
    https://stackoverflow.com/questions/62894666/path-and-name-of-exe-file-of-windows-terminal-preview

Create a shortcut, rename it to Terminal, and move it to:
    C:\ProgramData\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs

Right click on the shortcut, select properties and set `Shortcut key` to:
    CTRL + ALT + T
